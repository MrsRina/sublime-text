Neon Color Scheme v1.3.0
========================

Major changes for this version:
* Added support for logcat and RAML
* Properly highlight `Makefile` functions
* Tweaked highlighting of links in Markdown
* `comment.line.note[.notation]` now generic instead of Python-specific

Do you have a favorite plugin or language that you'd like to see themed by Neon?
Open an issue (https://github.com/MattDMo/Neon-color-scheme/issues/new)
and let me know!
